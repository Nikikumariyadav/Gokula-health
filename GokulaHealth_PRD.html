1. Executive Summary

Gokula Health is an offline-first Android application that serves as a digital health passport for dairy cattle. It enables small-scale farmers to register cattle, record daily milk yield, schedule vaccinations with automated reminders, and visualize production trends — all without requiring internet connectivity.

Built using Kotlin and Android Studio, the app addresses a critical gap in India's dairy sector by digitizing manual, oral record-keeping practices into an intuitive mobile solution designed specifically for rural farmers.

2. Problem Statement

India is the world's largest producer of milk, yet small-scale dairy farmers face significant income losses due to:

  • Missed Heat Cycles: Farmers miss the optimal 12–18 hour breeding window because there's no systematic tracking system.
  • Forgotten Vaccinations: Diseases like FMD (Foot & Mouth Disease), HS, and BQ cause livestock deaths that are 100% preventable with timely vaccination.
  • No Yield Analytics: Farmers cannot identify declining milk production trends without data, making it impossible to diagnose health issues early.
  • Oral Record-Keeping: All cattle health data is stored in memory or paper, leading to loss of historical records and inability to prove cattle health when selling or insuring animals.

📊 Impact Scale: Over 70 million smallholder dairy farmers in India manage cattle without digital tools. A 10% improvement in vaccination coverage and milk yield tracking could add ₹15,000+ annual income per farmer.

3. Solution Overview

Gokula Health transforms a farmer's smartphone into a comprehensive cattle management system. The app acts as a "Health Passport" — a permanent digital record for each animal that travels with it through its entire life cycle.

Core Value Propositions

  • ✅ Works completely offline — no internet required
  • ✅ Reminders work even after phone restart (BootReceiver)
  • ✅ Designed for low-tech users — emoji-based intuitive UI
  • ✅ Free — no subscription, no cloud costs
  • ✅ Data stored locally on device — private and secure

4. Feature Specifications

🐄 F1 — Cattle Profile Management

Register each cow with a unique Ear Tag ID, name, breed, date of birth, and photo from gallery or camera. Each profile serves as the cattle's permanent digital identity card.

  • Ear Tag ID validation (unique per animal)
  • Photo upload from gallery
  • Breed cataloging (HF, Jersey, Gir, Sahiwal, etc.)
  • Full CRUD — add, view, update, delete cattle

🥛 F2 — Milk Diary (Daily Yield Tracking)

Farmers record morning and evening milk yield in litres for each cow daily. The app automatically calculates the total daily yield and the monthly average.

  • Separate morning + evening entry fields
  • Auto-calculates total yield per day
  • Duplicate date protection — updates existing record if same date
  • Monthly average auto-calculated (meets project success criteria)

📊 F3 — Yield Graph (MPAndroidChart)

A 30-day bar chart visualizes milk production trends per cow, helping farmers instantly identify drops in production that may indicate health issues.

  • MPAndroidChart BarChart (last 30 days)
  • X-axis: Date labels (MM-DD format)
  • Y-axis: Total litres
  • Animated on load (800ms)

💉 F4 — Vaccination Scheduler with Auto-Reminders

Schedule vaccinations for FMD, HS, BQ, Anthrax, Brucellosis, IBR and others. The app automatically sets a device alarm 24 hours before the due date and sends a notification — works without internet.

  • Dropdown with 7 common vaccine types
  • AlarmManager fires reminder 1 day before due date
  • BootReceiver re-schedules alarms after device restart
  • Status tracking: Upcoming → Due Soon → Overdue → Completed
  • Color-coded alerts (Green / Orange / Red)

🏠 F5 — Dashboard (Home Screen)

A farmer-friendly dashboard showing all critical information at a glance: total cattle count, today's total milk yield across all cows, upcoming vaccinations this week, and overdue alerts.
